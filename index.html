About Conference

As the UI community in ThoughtWorks, we have always been playing with new tech and incubating ideas. So finally we have decided to share our stories and learning.

Unfold UI aims to become a platform for frontend developers to come together, share knowledge grow their network. We will have deep dives into latest industry trends and technologies in this space. From nifty CSS tricks to revisiting the core web concepts, there is something for everyone.

FAQs

Do I have to pay for attending the event?
Absolutely NO. It's a free UI community event.

I'm not from UI background, can I attend?
Yes please.

Can I bring my friend/colleague along?
Yes, let us know well before or ask them to register.

Can I get my laptop?
Yes, if you want to attend hands on workshops, please bring your own, we will update you with pre-requisites.
